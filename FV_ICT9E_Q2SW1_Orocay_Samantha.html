Pomeranians are distinguished by their luxurious double coat and foxy face with alert, prick ears. The body is fairly square, with a fluffy tail that curls up and over the back. A thick ruff around the neck completes the picture of a dog who can handle the northern German snows. The muzzle is distinct and the head is somewhat rounded. This elegant, very clever dog has a natural drive to work and is a fantastic student, which makes them popular as mountain rescue or sniffing dogs.
